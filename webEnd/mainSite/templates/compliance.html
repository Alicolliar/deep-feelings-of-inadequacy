Compliance Measures at Helium


This outlines the compliance measures the exchange attempts to take, to ensure transparency in operations.

We take a serious view to compliance here. We have logging systems in place, and once a week we are willing to give a full log of every trade and cash transaction to
the Department for Education and Commerce, as well as a full accounts and assets list. These will be deposited in the (hidden) compliance channels on our Discord server,
so please, if you are a representative of the DEC, please do join the server and we will give you access to these channels.
